About Me


My name is Jaycee. It has been a couple of minutes since my last cup of coffee and an undetermined amount of time since I last had a good night's rest... I attribute that to the fact that I had pre-existing insomnia before I decided to become a developer and now, I have insomnia caused by me trying to find a missing ; in my code.

Prior to becoming a developer, I was a stunt double for Ken Jeong. Unfortunately, Ken Jeong hasn't starred in any action films that have required him to use a stunt double so I've had to find work elsewhere.

On a more serious note, my real occupation was that of the Director of Admissions and Financial Aid for a local culinary school. I assisted students, many of whom were developers, in pursuing their culinary careers. When the school closed recently, I decided to follow the advice that I've given my students - pursue their passion. For me, it is coding.
